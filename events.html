Bayview Glen Team Sharing Event


On November 17, 2019, Ctrl-Z spent the afternoon with the 4 other Bayview Glen FLL teams to share practices which the team found beneficial this CITY SHAPERS season. We explained how programming the EV3 robot in Python was very efficient and fun, how using slow motion video can be used to debug code and troubleshoot the base robot and attachments and how certain team building activities can improve team alertness and enthusiasm prior to meetings and presentations. Thank you to our fellow BVG teams for sharing both their best practices and their progress this season.
